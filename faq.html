FAQ

How is this game played?
Go on location and tag it with your Street Art Gangs app
Collect points for your gang
Don't get busted!
The gang who has the most points when the game ends, wins!
How to get points?
You earn point by your actions. It is also possible to loose points.
Tagging a location gives 100p
Busting a member of another gang 30p
Changing status once a day 10p
Walk a kilometre within the game area 5p/km
Get busted! -30p
What is the game area?
For now it is nine blocks in the city center.
How do I start spraying?
Push icon in home screen
What "Bust" and "Busted" means?
You get busted when a member of a rival gang interrupts your tagging. You bust someone by interrupting them.
What can I do with this app?
Tag locations, follow your own and your gang's progress, set your mood from or real name from "Tweaks" and chat with your homies.
How to log out?
You stay logged in unless you log out. You can logout from "Tweaks"
